# Odds Notepad

A simple beginner web app for tracking odds from two sportsbooks.

## How to open

1. Open this folder on your computer.
2. Double-click `index.html` (or right-click and choose your browser).

## How to use

1. Enter values for **Game**, **Sportsbook A**, **Odds A**, **Sportsbook B**, and **Odds B**.
2. Click **Save** to add the entry to the table.
3. Entries are saved in your browser's local storage and remain after refresh.
4. Click **Delete** on any row to remove it.
